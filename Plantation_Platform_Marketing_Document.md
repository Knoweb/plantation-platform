## About the Platform
වැවිලි is a complete digital operating system for plantation estates. It modernizes labor, field, inventory, and approval workflows through one connected web platform designed for real operational environments.

Built as a **multi-tenant SaaS architecture**, the system supports multiple estates under one platform while preserving strict tenant-level data isolation and role-based access control.

## How It Works
The platform orchestrates estate operations through connected digital modules:

1. **Role-Based Access:** Users sign in to dashboards aligned with their responsibilities.
2. **Daily Operations Capture:** Field teams submit muster, output, and field activity data.
3. **Managerial Oversight:** Managers review approvals, monitor KPIs, and enforce norms.
4. **Inventory Fulfillment:** Store workflows process requisitions and update stock movement logs.
5. **Alerts & Collaboration:** Teams act quickly through real-time notifications and in-app correspondence.
